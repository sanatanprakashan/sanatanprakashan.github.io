Chetanya Foundation

Carrying forward the legacy of Sanatan Prakashan Mandir — preserving heritage, encouraging learning, and serving the community.

Our Mission

The Chetanya Foundation continues the values on which Sanatan Prakashan Mandir was founded in 1983 — authenticity, quality, and lasting value. Where the press once preserved knowledge through print, the Foundation works to preserve, digitise, and share that heritage with new generations.

From conserving historic documents and publications to supporting cultural and educational initiatives, the Foundation honours the work of Shri Krishna Ballabh Deen and those who carried it forward.

What We Do

Heritage

Preserving and digitising the books, documents, and photographs of the press so the legacy endures.

Learning

Encouraging reading, literature, and the study of religious and cultural works in the spirit of the Mandir.

Community

Sharing knowledge and supporting initiatives that bring people closer to their cultural roots.

Gopi Krishna Ballabh Pashu Sansthan

The Gopi Krishna Ballabh Pashu Sansthan is established in loving memory of the brothers Shri Gopi Ballabh Deen and Shri Krishna Ballabh Deen, whose names it carries forward.

Dedicated to the care, shelter, and welfare of animals, the Sansthan extends the family’s tradition of service—rooted in the same values of compassion, responsibility, and lasting good that have guided their work for generations.
